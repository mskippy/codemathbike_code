What We’ll Be Doing

  • Explore digital storytelling: how media influences communication and identity.
  • Design your personal brand: create a visual identity with logos, colour palettes, and typography.
  • Develop design skills: apply the CRAP principles—Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, Proximity.
  • Produce a podcast: plan, record, edit, and publish your own audio story.
  • Build an online showcase: create and share a personal portfolio website.
  • Practice digital citizenship: learn professional communication and ethical online conduct.

Tools and Platforms

  • Adobe Express, Canva, and Pixlr – for quick design and layout.
  • Audacity / Adobe Audition – for recording and editing audio.
  • Visual Studio Code – for web projects.
  • Microsoft Teams and OneDrive – for submitting and organizing work.
