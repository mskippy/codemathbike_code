Learning Goals

  • Collaborate effectively and name/organize files for a smooth workflow.

Mini-Lesson: Recording Foundations

  • Environment: choose a quiet, low-echo space (soft furnishings help).
  • Mic distance: about one hand-span from your mouth; stay consistent.
  • Monitor levels: aim for peaks around ‚Äì6 dB<>; always wear headphones.
  • Record in segments: plan short takes (30‚Äì60s) to make editing easier.
  • Name files clearly: lastname_firstname_test_room.wav<> or ep1_intro_raw.wav<>.
