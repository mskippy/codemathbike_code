Overview

In this unit, you will learn the fundamentals of game design by creating a platform game. The unit begins with a guided tutorial, then moves into designing and building your own game. By the end of the unit, you will have produced a functioning multi-level game.

  • Step 1: Follow a platform game tutorial to learn the core tools and mechanics.
  • Step 2: Create your own game by working through the design process:
    • Storyboard your game world and levels.
    • Define your characters, goals, enemies, and challenges.
    • Develop your game into multiple levels using GDevelop.
  • Assessment: You will be evaluated on the production and functionality of your final game.
