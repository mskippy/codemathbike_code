Upload Checklist

These must be in your OneDrive folder before your website is published:

  • ✔ index.html
  • ✔ about.html
  • ✔ game.html (with your trailer)
  • ✔ style.css (with your final colours & fonts)
  • ✔ Images folder (icons, banners, screenshots)
  • ✔ Trailer file (MP4) or working embed link
  • ✔ Game Main Character Icon PNG
How to Create Your Game Character Icon (PNG)

Your game icon should be a 300 × 300 px PNG. Follow these steps:

1️⃣ Create the Icon

  • Open Canva
  • Create a square design → 300 × 300 px
  • Add your main character or game symbol
  • Keep the design simple and bold

2️⃣ Export the Image

  • Click Share → Download
  • Select PNG
  • Download it as game_icon.png

3️⃣ Upload to Your Website Folder

Place the icon inside your website’s images folder:

/images/game_icon.png
How to Export Your Game Trailer (Clipchamp)

Your game trailer must be exported as an MP4 under 100 MB so it can be uploaded and published to your website.

1️⃣ Finish Your Edit

  • Make sure all clips, audio, and titles are complete.
  • Trim any extra seconds at the start or end.
  • Your trailer should be 20–60 seconds.

2️⃣ Export in Clipchamp

  • Click Export → 1080p
  • When the quality option appears, choose Medium
  • This keeps your file under 100 MB

3️⃣ Save Your File

Name your trailer exactly:

trailer.mp4

4️⃣ Upload to Your Website Folder

Place trailer.mp4 in the same folder as your other website files (next to index.html).

⭐ If Your File is Too Big

  • Re-export using 1080p → Medium
  • Or choose Low file size preset
  • Trailers should almost always be 20–60 MB

Your website will automatically show the trailer once everything is uploaded and published.
